About Us

Timuse Brownies crafts the best chocolate brownies using premium, regionally sourced ingredients. Every delivery of brownies is produced with a personal, homemade touch and is baked to order.

We provide a wide range of delectable flavor and combination options. We make conventional brownie boxes in a variety of sizes and quantities, personalized gift boxes, brownie stacks to accommodate larger gatherings and celebrations, and our incredibly decadent brownie slabs covered in a variety of tasty goodies.

Our baked brownies are delivered to any location in Batangas the very next day, and the prices of all of our products include delivery. There are no unstated fees; the price you see is the price you pay.

At room temperature, our homemade brownies will remain great for 2 weeks, and they may be frozen to keep them good for 3 months.

Please contact us to see how we can help you add luscious brownies to your menu or provide your next corporate gift with a customized touch.
